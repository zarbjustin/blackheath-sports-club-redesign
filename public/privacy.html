Privacy & cookies

This concept website is a non-commercial redesign proposal.

Blackheath Sports Club respects your privacy. This page explains what happens to your information when you use this website.

Information we collect

This website does not run analytics, advertising or tracking, and it does not set any cookies of its own. There are no sign-up or contact forms — enquiries are made through your own email or phone app when you choose to use the contact links.

Cookies and third-party content

The location map is provided by Google Maps and only loads when you choose to select “Load interactive map”. When you do, Google may set cookies and receive your IP address in line with its own privacy policy. If you do not load the map, no third-party cookies are set.

Links to other sites

Our sports sections (rugby, cricket, tennis and squash) and on-site partners (the gym and day nursery) link out to independent websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites.
